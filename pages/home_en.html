Project Till

Project Till aims at providing young/mobile internet entrepreneurs with a platform to showcase their products. This incubation project will also provide these entreprenurs with financial as well as technical managerial supports.

The process of this project is as follows: teams will be selected from online applications to participate in Shanghai Demo Day, those that participated in the Demo Day will be in the running to receive funding and some teams may be invited to come and work in the incubation office in Shanghai. Apart from an opportunity to realise your dreams and network with other entreprenurs, selected teams will benefit from the mentorship of established industry elites. These mentor includes: Hu Zemin, founder of 91.com; Cai Dongqing, Chairman of Alpha Animation; Liao Dong, co-founder of Forgame; Zhang Hongyu, co-founder of PPS and many others industry experts. Till Capital will also organize entrepreneur network events for those that didn't get to work with us in the incubation centre. These network events will be where these young entrepreneurs can mingle with investment team of Till Capital, founders of successful companies and industry experts, where they will receive expert guidance which will provide these young entrepreneurs with direction and advice.

Project team will begin in June, 2014 and it will end in December, 2015, with a duration of 18 months. The expected total capital investment for all teams is around 50 million RMB.

  Eligibility Criteria

  1. Must be in one of the following areas: internet gaming, internet education, internet health care, internet finance, wearable technology, smart home ware
  2. The founding entrepreneur(s) must be younger than 40 (including 40)
  3. The team must be smaller than 10 (including 10)
  4. The company must have a product prototype ready for demo
