About Till Capital

Positioning

Till Capital is positioned as an investment bank that caters specifically to internet start ups and offers a wide range of financial advisory services. We are primarily focused on 6 key aspects of internet driven industries: gaming, education, health care, finance, wearable technology and home ware. Our products include Till Capital, Investment Banking Online and Capital Circle are 3 complementary products that can provide a range of services for start up companies. These services include, financing and investing advisory, start up incubation, M&A advisory and others.

Till Capital is the venture investment arm of the firm, and also provides financing and after investment mentoring needs. Investment Bank Online is a platform for advertising and connecting incubated projects with potential investors. Capital Cirle completes the ecosystem by providing a mobile platform and crowd funding possibilities so the projects can reach a bigger audience.

Our mission is to disrupt and introduce new life into the methodologies of traditional banking services and facilitated a smooth investment banking experience for start ups.

Our Advantages

Complete database canvassing every aspect of the value chain. This includes fund managers, security analysts, investment banking sponsors and management of listed companies and other data.

Our team has fantastic execution ability being made up from experienced investment bankers and finance professionals. Our members are from Wall St, Deloitt, KPMG, IDG Capital and top securities, venture capital and accounting firms in China.

We offer one stop shop investment banking services. We can cater to investment and finance needs along the entire value chain. Through Till Capital, Investment Banking Online and Capital Circle, we can provide a one stop shop for entrepreneurs.

Fund Size

Till Capital Fund 1 has a size of 100 million RMB. Cornerstone investors comes from listed companies, industrialists, successful internet entrepreneurs and investment bankers.
