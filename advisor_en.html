Industrial Advisors

Qingdong Cai

Mr. Cai is the Chairman of Alpha Animation Co., Limited. He received EMBA from Zhongshan University. Mr. Cai is among the top entrepreneurs in China, who is an expert of industrial structure. Starting from toy products and developing anime and film as core business, Mr. Cai successfully paved the way for Alpha Animation to create a complete animation industrial chain, which includes animation production, media disseminating, image authorization, product design and marketing. Alpha Animation is the first listed animation producer with the largest market value in China. It also owns the largest number of cartoon characters. Alpha Animation’s product line includes animation, film, video, Internet game, online education, toys, children products, anime derivatives and other relevant products. The company’s goal is to become the Chinese "Disney" and disseminate the outstanding Chinese culture all over the world.
